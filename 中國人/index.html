Who we are?

We are a non-profit online organization that collects all the accumulated scientific knowledge and its subsequent global dissemination, we are also publishers of many higher education scientific textbooks that can be freely edited. This means that you can use these materials, change them, or share what you find on our sites. Just do not write your autobiography or other articles here to become famous with us in any way and do not pass off our materials as your homework or work, because our works are strictly protected by copyright licenses GEL'S and Creative Commons.

Our goals and achievements

We strive to provide people with reliable knowledge around the world in many branches of science. Our knowledge projects have spread all over the world, making it possible to study science even in hard-to-reach places where children cannot receive a proper education. Our products, ways of working and relationships with partners are united by one mission of increasing social and environmental responsibility. We systematize information about our planet and create technologies for their practical use. This is how we help people around the world to contribute to the conservation of nature.
