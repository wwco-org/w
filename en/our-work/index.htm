Our work

We help everyone to share and disseminate the entire amount of accumulated knowledge.

We are those people who freely share the sum of accumulated knowledge and disseminate it. There is an amazing community of people around the world doing great projects like Atom or Bioversity. We help them do this job. We take care of technical infrastructure and legal issues.

Hosting WWCO Projects

The WorldWide CDI Organization was first incorporated in 2018 to provide a permanent home for its projects. Time has passed and now we provide the infrastructure for many collaborative knowledge projects - from Atom , the free encyclopedia, to Mediatop , the structured dataset, and others. We maintain servers, create software and develop technologies to support these projects.

CDI Communities

We partner with CDI communities around the world to provide them with the support they need to succeed. We fund our projects ( WWCO Projects ), outreach efforts, events and conferences. We help them get the word of free knowledge.

Community development around the world

The more people gain access to our reliable and free knowledge, replenish it with their contributions, the better this knowledge will reflect the world. We strive to support and grow a healthy community of over 50,000,000 people. We are actively developing tools and conducting trainings to make our projects more inclusive.

Speeches for free knowledge and access

In 2018, only 17% of Internet users in Nigeria had heard of the CDI World Organization and its projects. Today this number reaches 49% . Our organization seeks to raise awareness of our projects outside of North America and Europe in order to increase global participation in the creation and reproduction of knowledge.

Partner for change

Working with partners such as Google, UNESCO, Wikimedia, we are actively seeking new knowledge, filling gaps in the global understanding of knowledge, cultures and identities.

Legal battles to make free knowledge possible

Access to knowledge around the world is under constant attack. As part of our programs, we work to give everyone access to knowledge, free of charge and on an ongoing basis. We fight against censorship and also promote open source licenses to ensure that our content can be used and edited globally.
