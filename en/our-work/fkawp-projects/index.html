Free Knowledge Alliance of WWCO Projects (FKAWP)

Table of Contents

FKAWP is an alliance of free knowledge projects by the WorldWide CDI Organization, engaged in the implementation of projects aimed at achieving a high level of world online education.


The WorldWide CDI Organization supports hundreds of thousands of people around the world in creating the largest free knowledge projects in history. Volunteer work helps millions of people around the world to find information, contribute their knowledge and share it with others, regardless of their ability.

Encyclopedias

  • Atom

    All knowledge of nuclear energy in the world

    Atom — is a free, collaborative encyclopedia of nuclear energy, written by volunteers around the world.

  • Andromeda

    Online encyclopedia of outer space

    Andromeda aims to create a major online encyclopedia of astronomy, including textbooks, annotated texts, study guides and manuals.

  • Bioverse

    Online encyclopedia on biological aspects

    Bioverse aims to create a major online encyclopedia of biology, including textbooks, annotated texts, study guides and manuals.
