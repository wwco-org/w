How the idea of creating the concept of WWCO and the WorldWide CDI Organization appeared?

By Dmitriy Savkin•12 January 2022
        
Flag of the World CDI Organization adopted by the WWCO government in 2021.

Introduction

“WorldWide CDI Organization is an organization dedicated to online education, whose main function is educational content and its global distribution, and considers its mission to be free access to knowledge for everyone..”

CDI Ministry of Information, WWCO Government

In such a short period of time, the CDI World Organization has already become a giant in the field of online education and the dissemination of free knowledge on the Internet. Free knowledge organizations and projects such as Wikimedia, BDT, Scholarpedia, and others create a global community that is constantly growing and evolving. Among the many "material" encyclopedias, online encyclopedias occupy a special place, because the pages on them will never end, and the monotonous "cover" will cease to be such: thousands of editors are constantly working to improve both existing articles and create new ones that are useful and sometimes special. At a time when moderators and people responsible for the site design will improve its "cover", i.e. its appearance and further. The idea of creating free knowledge projects on the Internet a couple of decades ago might have seemed unpromising, but as we know, times are changing, and even those ideas that initially might have seemed strange and meaningless are no longer so. Even today, the number of volunteer editors of free knowledge is more than 500,000, and visiting WWCO or Wikipedia projects per second increases 6000 people, just this one thought surprises and inspires at the same time, doesn't it?

Creation idea

The idea of creating a business came to me spontaneously, without any reason, of course, I didn't immediately come to free knowledge projects, and I didn't even think about creating them. My first business idea was to create an online magazine on the topic of entertainment and partly interesting facts and events. All the previous projects and companies that I founded earlier had either little or no success at all, so they had to be closed. However, with the establishment of a small company "CDI Foundation", things started to improve, we gained hundreds of readers in just a month, and after 4 years we developed a global community, a single global organization that has provided, provides and will continue to provide high-quality and meaningful educational information for all these 4 years. Over the past 4 years, we have managed to gain much more than hundreds of readers, at the moment our projects are visited by about 3-4 billion readers from dozens of countries a year, such as: Russia, Belarus, Ukraine, Kazakhstan, Poland, the USA, the Netherlands, Spain, Austria and others. This figure is definitely huge, and we will continue to improve!
