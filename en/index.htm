Who we are?

The WorldWide CDI Organization (WWCO) is an online education organization whose main function lies in educational content and its global distribution. We invite everyone who shares our vision to join us in collecting and disseminating knowledge that fully represents the diversity of humanity.

1 Who we are?

We are a non-profit online organization that collects all the accumulated scientific knowledge, and its subsequent global distribution, we are also publishers of many scientific manuals of higher education, which can be freely edited. This means that you can use these materials, change them, or share what you find on our sites. Just do not write your autobiography or other articles here in order to become famous with us in any way and do not pass off our materials as your homework or work, because our works are strictly protected by copyright licensesGEL'S and Creative Commons.

2 We respect your data

We do not sell your email address or any of your personal information to third parties. You can learn more about our practice of handling your confidential data from theproduct use conditions of the WorldWide CDI Organization.

3 Our goals and achievements

Our products, working methods and relationships with partners are united by one mission of increasing social and environmental responsibility for all. We organize information about our planet and create technologies for their practical use. This is how we help people around the world to contribute to the conservation of nature.
