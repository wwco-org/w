14 June
A decision was made to change a number of features in relation to the "Bioversity" project.
Today it was decided to change a number of features in relation to the "Bioversity" project:
1. The project will change its former name from "Bioversity" to "Sphere".
2. The project will partially abandon the services of "MediaWiki" and accordingly change its old design to a new one.
3. The project, after changing its name, will change the domain name.
After the expiration of the conversion of the "Sphere" encyclopedia on the previous domain, a link to the current encyclopedia will be provided for 1 week, after which it will be completely disabled.
