EN
Donation ($) can be made by: Cards - Visa, MasterCard, Maestro. Cryptocurrencies - Bitcoin, Ethereum, Tether, Litecoin. Payment systems - QIWI, Advcash, PerfectMoney. Donations (other currencies) can be supported in many ways including those described above, follow the instructions described in the application.
