We ask you to help spread free knowledge

Let's get straight to the point: today we ask you to protect and support our projects.

We are a non-profit organization that depends in part on donations, but 98% of our readers don't donate; they just look the other way.

When we made our projects knowledge non-commercial, people told us that we would regret it. But if such global knowledge projects as Wikipedia, BDT, FKAWP Alliance Projects and many others became commercial, it would be a great loss for the whole world.

Therefore, FKAWP projects are a place for learning, not advertising. The heart and soul of our projects is a community of people working to provide you with unlimited access to reliable and neutral information.

We know that most people will ignore this message. But if we're even a little bit helpful to you, consider donating $15, $30, $70 or whatever you want to help protect and support FKAWP projects.

Thank you,

Dmitry Savkin
Founder of FKAWP Projects and WorldWide CDI Organization.

Tax deductibility information

We do not sell or trade your information to anyone. By donating, you agree to share your personal information with the WorldWide CDI Organization, the nonprofit organization that hosts FKAWP Projects, and its service providers. For recurring donors, fixed monthly payments will be debited by the WorldWide CDI Organization on the monthly anniversary of the first donation, until such time as you notify us to discontinue them. Donations initiated on the 29, 30, or 31 of the month will recur on the last day of the month for shorter months, as close to the original date as possible.
