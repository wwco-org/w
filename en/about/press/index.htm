WWCO Facts & Figures

  • The Atom and Andromeda Encyclopedias were launched in 2019.
  • Currently there are more than 50 million participants in WWCO projects.
  • WWCO's projects and programs have over 2 billion readers a month.
  • More than 800,000 editors contribute to WWCO projects every month.
  • WWCO Projects are visited and read over 23 billion times a month. This means that people visit our projects about 15,000 times per second.
  • The CDI World Organization websites are visited monthly from over 3 billion unique devices.
  • Due to our strong commitment to privacy, we do not track users of our sites.
  • We have information on the number of unique devices from which WWCO sites and projects are visited.
