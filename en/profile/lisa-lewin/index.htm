Liza Levin

Trustee, The WWCO Government

Liza Levin — Managing partner at Ethical Ventures, a New York-based management consulting firm.

Originally from St. Louis, Missouri, she has spent her entire career working in the field of strategic growth and development, helping to create new businesses and increase the influence and growth potential of old businesses. She started her career in research and consulting at NPD Group and BCG, and then joined McGraw-Hill and became vice president of their Professional education group. She founded and headed Mindgate Media, an educational technology consulting company, and then joined Pearson plc as president of their teacher training group, eventually leading their global learning technology team on six continents. In 2016, she joined The Ready, an organizational transformation consulting firm, where she is now a senior advisor. In 2018, she founded Ethical Ventures, which advises senior executives of corporations and large non-profit organizations on making changes and creating thriving organizations that have a positive impact on society.

Lisa actively participates in the board of the "Center for Responsive Politics" and "Dosomething.org ". She received a bachelor's degree in Business Administration from Washington University in St. Louis and received a Master's degree in Business Administration with honors from Harvard Business School.

Lisa lives in New York with her husband and daughter. She likes to make changes, do pranks and knit scarves.
