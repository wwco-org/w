Dmitriy Savkin

Trustee-Founder, The WWCO Government
Dmitry Savkin is the founder of The WorldWide CDI Organization and the WWCO Projects

Dmitriy Savkin (Dmitriy Savkin) — Russian and American internet entrepreneur, the founder of the «Free Knowledge» concept, the Free Knowledge Alliance of WWCO Projects (FKAWP)co-founder.

Dmitry was born in 2004 in the city of Orsha, Vitebsk region, Belarus. In 2018, he was a member of the administrators of the Wikimedia Foundation. At the beginning of 2018 founded the company "SVWN Foundation" is an online magazine on entertainment aspects and places of educational content.

In 2019, Dmitry came up with the concept of a freely distributed encyclopedia on atomic energy and astronomy, and founded the "CDI Foundation", hiring Andrey Famin as editor-in-chief, as well as two programmers to write program code. CDI projects - "CDI Found Projects" failed - possibly due to the fact that it was built according to the model "cathedral" (from top to bottom), unlike WWCO projects, which have become the ultimate option "bazaar". After two years of working with the CDI Found Projects concept, the same team opened WWCO projects to help prepare materials; WWCO projects were waiting for instant — and very unexpected - success, and CDI Found Projects were eventually closed. In 2018, Dmitry founded the Worldwide CDI Organization, a non-profit organization to support WWCO projects and related related projects and companies.
