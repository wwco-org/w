Donation

To confirm your donation request, you must contact: mail.wwco@gmail.com indicating your full name, amount and currency.

We ask you to comply with our requirements, this is done in order to ensure the safety of users, in accordance with our law, which we adopted in September 2020.

Notes

Please note that the WorldWide CDI Organization cannot refund donations. Network and miner fees are non-refundable. WorldWide CDI Organization is not responsible for any changes in value caused by fluctuations in exchange rates.

For questions, please contact mail.wwco@gmail.com.

The WorldWide CDI Organization (WWCO) is an online education organization whose main function lies in educational content and its global distribution, and considers its mission to be free access to knowledge for every person.
